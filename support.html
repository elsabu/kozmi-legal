Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Kozmi Pro subscriptions are managed through Apple. On your iPhone, go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your subscription.

I don't know my exact birth time — what should I do?

If you don't know your birth time, Kozmi can still generate an approximate birth chart, though details like your Rising sign and house positions may not be fully accurate. We recommend checking your birth certificate or contacting your local registry office.

I'm not receiving notifications — what should I do?

Check that notifications are enabled on your iPhone under Settings → Notifications → Kozmi. If the issue persists, reach out to us.
