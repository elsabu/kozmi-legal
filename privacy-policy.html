Kozmi ("the app", "we") cares about your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use Kozmi, how it is used, who it may be shared with, and what rights you have over your data.

The data controller for Kozmi is Elif Sayan Bulut (independent developer, based in Turkey).

1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to all users of the Kozmi iOS app (bundle ID: com.kozmiapp.ios). By downloading and using the app, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

3. How we use your data

  • To calculate and deliver your natal chart and personalized astrological content,
  • To generate daily content, transit insights, and Discover cards,
  • To verify and secure your account,
  • To manage your subscription and purchases,
  • To send you push notifications (with your consent),
  • To improve the app and fix bugs,
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5. AI-generated content

Kozmi uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate personalized daily insights, transit analyses, and other content for you. As part of this process, astrological data derived from your birth chart (such as planetary positions and house placements) is sent to this API for the purpose of generating personalized text. This data is processed under Anthropic's own data-handling terms and is not used to train models (under the commercial terms of the Anthropic API).

6. The Connections feature and shared data

When you add someone via the "Connections" feature, you are responsible for entering their birth information. You should not add another person's personal data without their explicit consent.

The Connections feature allows a synastry (compatibility) analysis to be generated between two birth charts. This analysis is visible only to the user who created the connection; if the added person does not have a Kozmi account, this data is not shared with them and they are not notified.

7. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. When you delete your account, your personal data (birth information, connection data, usage history) is permanently deleted from our systems within a reasonable period. Certain transaction records (such as purchase history) may need to be retained longer to comply with legal obligations.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know whether your data is being processed,
  • The right to request information about the data we hold about you,
  • The right to request correction of your data,
  • The right to request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements,
  • The right to object to processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 13, or delete your account directly from within the app.

9. Children's privacy

Kozmi is not designed for or directed at individuals under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If we believe you are under 16, we may suspend or close your account.

10. Data security

We use encryption and access-control mechanisms (Row Level Security) provided by Supabase to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

11. International data transfers

The service providers we use (Supabase, Anthropic, RevenueCat, Apple, Google, Expo) may process your data on servers located outside of Turkey (for example, in the EU or the US). These transfers take place under each provider's own data protection standards.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by email. The current version will always be available at this page.
